Welcome to FitnessApp.

Who are we?

FitnessApp is a group-focused fitness application that allows you to create and participate in fitness challenges. Choose between private and public challenges and show off your successful completions to your friends!

Why choose us?

If you have ever felt unmotivated to get off the couch and exercise, or put down the junk food and eat healthy, FitnessApp is perfect for you. We provide you the perfect platform to push health & fitness beyond the individual level, and dive into its social aspect. Eat healthy, get fit, and have fun while doing it!

Features

We currently support two types of challenges: a nutrition goal, and a running goal. Stay tuned for more coming soon!
